Our Story

Piyal Stores (Pvt) Ltd is a Sri Lankan company specializing in exporting high-quality spices, grains, and agricultural commodities. Founded on the principles of quality, integrity, and sustainability, we connect the hard work of Sri Lankan farmers with the global market.

Under the leadership of our Managing Director, W.A.K. Piyal Ranjith, we have grown into a trusted name in the international spice trade, known for our uncompromising standards and reliable delivery.

"Our goal is to ensure that every grain and spice we export represents the rich heritage and superior quality of Sri Lankan agriculture."

— W.A.K. Piyal Ranjith, Managing Director

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Sri Lankan agricultural products to the world while supporting local farming communities through sustainable trade practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading global partner for Sri Lankan spices and commodities, setting the standard for quality and ethical exports.
